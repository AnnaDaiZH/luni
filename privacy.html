Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective immediately

Short version: Luni stores all your data on your own device and in your personal iCloud account. We have no servers, we collect no personal data, and we never sell or share your information. Your baby's data belongs to you.

1. Who We Are

Luni is developed and operated by Anna Dai, based in Zürich, Switzerland ("we", "us", "our"). Contact: hello@getluni.ch

2. What Data We Collect

We collect no personal data ourselves. Luni does not have a backend server. All data you enter into the app is stored exclusively in two places:

  • Your device — locally via Core Data on your iPhone
  • Your iCloud account — synced via Apple's CloudKit when you enable partner sharing

This means your data never touches our servers, because we don't have any.

3. Partner Sharing

When you invite a partner to share data, Luni uses Apple's CloudKit sharing (CKShare) to share your data directly between iCloud accounts. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to the shared data.

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

Luni uses Apple's built-in analytics (if you have opted in to share analytics with app developers in your iPhone settings). This provides us with anonymised, aggregated crash reports and usage statistics. No personally identifiable information is included. You can opt out in:

iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements → Share with App Developers

5. In-App Purchases

Premium unlocks are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. All purchase data is handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Luni is designed for use by parents and caregivers — not by children. The app tracks information about infants, but children do not use the app directly. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Health-Related Data

Luni allows you to record health-related information about your baby (growth measurements, feeding, sleep). This data is stored exclusively on your device and iCloud account, as described above. We do not access, transmit, or process this data.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / Swiss DSG)

As a user in Switzerland or the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since all your data is stored in your iCloud account and on your device, you can:

  • Delete all app data by deleting the Luni app from your device
  • Manage iCloud data at appleid.apple.com
  • Contact us at hello@getluni.ch for any privacy questions

9. Data Transfers

No personal data is transferred to us or our servers. Data synced via iCloud is subject to Apple's data handling practices. Apple may store iCloud data in data centres in Europe or the United States, subject to their privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via an app update notice. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at hello@getluni.ch
