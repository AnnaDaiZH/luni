Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026 · Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Short version: Luni stores all your data on your own device and in your personal iCloud account. We have no servers, collect no analytics, and never sell or share your information. Your baby's data belongs to you.

Anna Dai ("we," "our," or "us") built Luni as a free app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Luni. By using Luni, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Caregiver name: When you first open the app, you provide a display name (alias) to identify yourself as a caregiver. This name is stored locally and shared with your partner through iCloud sync.
  • Baby information: You provide your baby's name, date of birth, and optional details during onboarding. This information is stored locally and synced via iCloud.
  • Care event data: You log feeding, sleep, diaper, and growth events for your baby. This data is stored locally on your device and synced via Apple iCloud (CloudKit).
  • Support requests: If you contact us for support, we collect your email address and the content of your message.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your email address, phone number, or Apple ID.
  • We do not require account creation — the app uses your existing iCloud account for sync.
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.
  • We do not use crash reporting services.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect location, health, camera, contacts, or any device sensor data.
  • We do not use advertising or ad-tracking identifiers.

iCloud Sync (CloudKit)

Luni uses Apple's CloudKit service to sync your data between your devices and with your partner. This means:

  • Your care event data is stored in Apple's iCloud servers under your Apple ID.
  • When you share with a partner, shared data is accessible to both Apple IDs through CloudKit's sharing mechanism.
  • Apple processes this data according to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not operate our own servers and do not have direct access to your iCloud data.

Local Storage

  • Preferences: App settings (appearance mode, time format) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. These are not synced or transmitted.
  • Partner aliases: Display names you assign to your partner are stored locally and not shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Provide Luni's core functionality — tracking feeding, sleep, diaper changes, and growth
  • Sync your data across your devices and with your partner via iCloud
  • Display caregiver names alongside care events

We do NOT use your information to sell your data, build advertising profiles, perform analytics, or make automated decisions that affect you.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Your partner: When you use the sharing feature, your care event data is shared with the partner you invite via iCloud. You control who has access.
  • Apple iCloud: Your data is stored and synced through Apple's CloudKit service, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.

4. Third-Party Services

Luni uses the following third-party service:

Apple iCloud (CloudKit)Data sync between devices and partnersApple Privacy Policy

We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

5. Data Retention

  • Care event data: Retained as long as you use the app. You may delete individual events at any time within the app.
  • Caregiver and baby information: Retained as long as you use the app. Deleting the app removes local data. iCloud data can be managed through your iCloud settings.
  • Local preferences: Stored on device only. Deleted when you delete the app.

To delete all your data:

  1. Delete individual events within the app
  2. Delete the app from your device (removes local data)
  3. Remove Luni data from iCloud via Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage

6. Data Security

  • All data synced via iCloud is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Local data is stored in the app's sandboxed container, protected by iOS security
  • No data is transmitted to any server other than Apple's iCloud

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we rely on Apple's industry-standard security infrastructure for data protection.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data directly within the app at any time.
  • Correction: Edit any care event or profile information within the app.
  • Deletion: Delete individual events or all data as described in Section 5.
  • Portability: Your data is stored in your iCloud account, which you control.
  • Withdraw consent: Stop using the app and delete your data at any time.

To exercise any rights not available through the app, contact us at hello@getluni.ch.

For European Union / Swiss Residents (GDPR / DSG)

If you are located in the EEA or Switzerland, you have additional rights:

  • Data controller: Anna Dai, hello@getluni.ch
  • Lawful basis: Performance of a contract; your consent (for partner sharing, revocable at any time)
  • Right to restrict processing, object, and lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority
  • Data transfers: Processed by Apple iCloud with Standard Contractual Clauses. See Apple's commitment to privacy.

For California Residents (CCPA)

  • We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • You have the right to know, delete, and opt-out. Contact hello@getluni.ch to exercise your rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Luni is designed for use by parents and caregivers — not by children. The app is not intended for use by children under 13. While the app stores information about babies and infants, this data is provided and controlled by the parent or caregiver. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@getluni.ch.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and notify you through the app for significant changes. Continued use of Luni after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
